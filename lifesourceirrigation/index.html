Services

Declan Keane brings 20 years of irrigation experience, a degree in agricultural science from the University College Dublin, and a love of all things green to Lifesource Irrigation, serving the best residential and commercial gardens in New York City.

We specialize in drip irrigation systems for backyards, rooftops, penthouses, and apartment buildings. Plenty of experience with systems installed at some houses over twenty years. Experts in both indoor and outdoor drip irrigation. Also installed lawn sprinklers of many varieties.

We install and maintain low voltage landscape lighting for all terraces and backyards, with switches, timers, and a wide variety of fixtures.

Our experienced staff is highly trained and can help in all aspects of irrigation, lighting and maintenance including installation, design, maintenance, winterizing, spring set up, renovations of old system, hiding water lines and lighting wire.

About Declan

Born and raised on a farm in Kilnamona, Co. Clare, Ireland, schooled locally and in the big city of Ennis by Christian Brothers. After high school, he moved to Galway for one year to the University College Galway and then next to Dublin to get an Agricultural Science degree and higher diploma in Education at University College Dublin. He worked as a teacher and at a soil assessment office for a couple of years before coming to New York on March 25th, 1988. With some teaching, some landscaping, and a lot of hand watering, he fell into the irrigation business. Estelle Irrigation was formed and he set about bringing drip irrigation to all New York City rooftops (a very new idea at the time).
