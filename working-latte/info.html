So the story goes...

In September 2013, I moved to Brooklyn, specifically the Boerum Hill neighborhood. Choosing which neighborhood to live to call home can be a difficult decision and NYC can be especially challenging.

On the weekends, my one of favorite ways to explore the NYC is on foot NYC and I love coffee. I wanted to explore the neighborhoods by foot and would find a coffee shop (usually via FourSquare or Yelp) and after a walk want to work a little.

At times, I got frustrated because the popular spots usually have a no laptop policies on the weekend, which is understandable, but usually not listed on Foursquare or the shop's website. So this site was born out that frustration. It's also my final (work-in-progress) project for my Front-End Web Development (aka FEWD) taught by General Assembly.

In the meantime, checkout Workfrom.
