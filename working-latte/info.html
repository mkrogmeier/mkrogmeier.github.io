So the story goes...

In September 2013, I moved to Brooklyn, specifically the Boerum Hill neighborhood.

My favorite way to explore NYC, or any city for that matter, is on foot and I've found that an afternnon in a coffee shop is like a window into the character of the neighborhod. So on weekends, I'd find a coffee shop (usually via FourSquare or Yelp) in a new part of town to give me a starting point or and end destination for my walks.

And sometimes, I simply wanted a fun place to work and enjoy a latte.

I quickly became frustrated because popular coffee shops usually have a no laptop policies on the weekends, understandablely, but that info wasn't listed on Foursquare or the shop's website. So this site was born out that frustration. It's also my final (work-in-progress) project for my Front-End Web Development (aka FEWD) taught by General Assembly.

In the meantime, checkout Workfrom.
